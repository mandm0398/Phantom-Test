Mousetrap Car Project

As part of my Introduction to Mechanical Engineering class I was tasked with creating a mousetrap powered vehical capable of traveling 12 ft over terrain consisting of 1in by 2in speed bumps in the fastest time possible. This task was completed through various iterations which eventually resulted in the creation of the fastest car in the class.

Prototypes

1st Prototype

As a large acceleration was needed at the beginning of the race but not towards the end, we created a variable axle which began at a larger diameter to provide more torque and then became smaller to provide a faster angular velocity.
